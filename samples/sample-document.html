Company Overview

TechVision Innovations Inc. is a leading technology company founded in 2018 by Dr. Sarah Chen and Michael Rodriguez in San Francisco, California. The company specializes in artificial intelligence solutions, cloud computing services, and enterprise software development.

Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge technology solutions that drive innovation and growth. We currently employ 2,847 people across 15 offices worldwide, including locations in New York, London, Tokyo, Singapore, and Sydney.
