## üìù How to Test

1. **Convert to PDF:**
   - Open `sample-document.txt` in any text editor
   - Print/Export as PDF
   - Or use online converter: https://www.ilovepdf.com/txt_to_pdf

2. **Upload to Vector AI:**
   - Go to http://localhost:4200
   - Drag & drop the PDF file
   - Wait for indexing to complete

3. **Ask Questions:**
   - Copy questions from above
   - Paste into the search box
   - Compare answers with expected results

---

## üéØ Testing Tips

- **Simple Questions:** Start with straightforward factual questions
- **Complex Questions:** Try combining multiple topics
- **Paraphrasing:** Ask the same question in different ways
- **Specific vs General:** Test both detailed and broad questions

### Example Complex Questions:
- "Compare the revenue from Cloud Services and AI Solutions"
- "What benefits do employees receive at TechVision?"
- "Summarize the company's environmental initiatives"
- "What makes SecureData Shield unique?"
